About Me

Hi, my name is Rianne Camille I am here to admit that I am a make-up addict =). My passion for the arts began at a very young age, where I spent my childhood sketching on drawing pads or painting on the walls before I even learned to write. As a preteen, make-up became an exciting outlet to express my creativity. I was instantly captivated by the concept of make-up as an art form, with a fresh face as my blank canvas. Since then, my love affair with make-up began.

Rianne's Career

Dedication, passion, and the eagerness to learn & adapt are just a few components to Rianne's professionalism in the make-up world. Rianne Camille started her beauty career in her early teens, taking on friends and family as clients as freelance work. In 2008, she became a make-up artist for MAC COSMETICS and has worked at both San Francisco and Los Angeles regions. Rianne's exceptional talent and artistry was recognized by the company which led to a promoted managerial position in Glendale, California. With her advanced certifications earned within the company, she helped enrich the training and development of other make-up artists and also led classes for clients. After five years with MAC, Rianne flourished a freelance career path to further expand her experience and brush strokes.
